HomeAbout Us

Welcome to Afdal Setup UAE, your premier partner in business establishment and growth in one of the world's most dynamic markets. As part of the renowned Afdal Group, we specialize in providing comprehensive support for individuals and companies looking to navigate the complexities of starting a new business in Dubai. Our mission is to simplify the business setup process, empowering you to focus on what matters most-growing your venture.

Our Vision

At Afdal Setup UAE, we envision a thriving business ecosystem where entrepreneurs and businesses can flourish. Dubai's strategic location, robust infrastructure, and favorable business climate make it an ideal place for new ventures. We are committed to helping you harness these opportunities by offering tailored solutions that meet your unique needs.

Why Choose Afdal Group?

  1. Expertise and Experience: With years of experience in the UAE market, our team of experts understands the intricacies of business setup in Dubai. We keep up with the latest regulations, market trends, and best practices, ensuring that you receive the most relevant and effective guidance.
  2. Comprehensive Services: Starting a new business in Dubai can be overwhelming. That's why we offer a full range of services to support you at every stage of your journey, including:
    • Business Licensing: We assist you in navigating the licensing process, ensuring compliance with local laws and regulations.
    • Company Formation: Whether you are looking to establish a sole proprietorship, a limited liability company (LLC), or a free zone entity, our consultants guide you through the entire formation process.
    • Market Entry Strategies: Our team provides insights tailored to your industry, helping you effectively enter and compete in the Dubai market.
    • Office Solutions: We help you find the ideal office space to suit your business model and budget, facilitating a smooth operational setup.
    • Visa Services: We simplify the visa application process for you and your employees, ensuring a hassle-free transition to Dubai.
  3. Personalized Approach: We understand that every business is unique, and so are its challenges. Our approach is personalized, taking the time to understand your specific goals and tailoring our services accordingly. This ensures that you receive solutions that align perfectly with your vision.
  4. Strong Network: As part of the Afdal Group, we have established strong relationships with local authorities and industry professionals. This network enables us to expedite processes and provide you with the best resources for your business setup.
